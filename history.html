Our History

The journey of PRHInc from vision to impact

The PRHInc Story

PRHInc (Palayan Recruitment Hub Incorporated) began with a simple but powerful vision: to provide free, high-quality call center training to the youth of Palayan City and Nueva Ecija. What started as a small initiative has grown into a recognized training institution that has helped hundreds of young Filipinos launch successful careers in the BPO industry.

Our journey reflects the growing importance of the BPO sector in the Philippines and the untapped potential of talent in the provinces. By bringing world-class training to Palayan City, we've shown that geographical location should not be a barrier to career success.

Key Milestones

Important moments in our journey

2013

PCIT Established

The Palayan City Institute of Technology (PCIT) was established by the city government as the premier technical and vocational institution of Palayan City. Accredited by TESDA, PCIT began offering programs in Caregiving, Welding, and other technical courses.

2014

Call Center Training Wave 5

On January 20, 2014, PCIT launched Call Center Training Wave 5 under the leadership of Mayor Adrianne Mae Cuevas. This marked the beginning of structured BPO training in Palayan City, with 20 initial trainees learning fundamentals of call center operations.

2015

Partnership with Sutherland

PRHInc established a formal partnership with Sutherland Global Services, enabling direct endorsement of top-performing graduates for employment. This partnership became a cornerstone of our training program's value proposition.

2016

Expanded Training Programs

Following the success of our initial batches, we expanded our training curriculum to include advanced modules on technical support, healthcare accounts, and non-voice BPO operations.

2018

100th Graduate Milestone

PRHInc celebrated its 100th graduate, with a significant majority finding employment in various BPO companies across Central Luzon and Metro Manila. The program's reputation for producing job-ready candidates grew exponentially.

2020

Virtual Training Adaptation

In response to the pandemic, PRHInc quickly adapted by launching virtual training sessions via Zoom, ensuring that aspiring BPO professionals could continue their training safely from home.

2022

Mayor Viandrei Nicole J. Cuevas Support

Under the new leadership of Mayor Viandrei Nicole J. Cuevas, the city government renewed its commitment to supporting PRHInc's mission, providing enhanced facilities and resources for training.

2024

Digital Transformation

PRHInc embraced digital transformation with the launch of our online registration system, website, and digital training resources, making our programs more accessible to youth across Nueva Ecija.

2026

Continuing the Legacy

Today, PRHInc continues to train the next generation of BPO professionals, with enhanced curriculum, experienced trainers, and strong industry partnerships. Our graduates are making their mark in the Philippine BPO industry.
