Your 10-Day Training Journey

Here's what happens each day of training

Day 1

Welcome & Orientation

Meet your trainers and fellow trainees. Get oriented to the training facility, understand the program structure, and set your personal goals for the 10-day journey.

Day 2

BPO Industry Deep Dive

Understand the BPO landscape in the Philippines. Learn about different types of accounts (voice, non-voice, healthcare, technical support), salary ranges, and career progression paths.

Day 3

Communication Foundations

Build your communication skills with exercises in clarity, tone, and pacing. Practice pronunciation and learn professional vocabulary used in call center operations.

Day 4

Customer Service Mastery

Learn the art of customer service including active listening, empathy, problem resolution, and handling difficult customers with professionalism.

Day 5

Call Handling Workshop

Master call handling techniques from opening to closing. Learn proper call flow, documentation, and how to use CRM systems effectively.

Day 6

Accent & Pronunciation

Focus on neutral accent development and pronunciation. Practice with tongue twisters, conversation drills, and phonetic exercises.

Day 7

Mock Call Practice

Begin hands-on mock calling sessions. Practice real scenarios with your trainer providing real-time feedback and coaching.

Day 8

Advanced Scenarios

Tackle challenging call scenarios including complaints, escalations, and technical issues. Build confidence in handling complex situations.

Day 9

Interview Preparation

Learn interview techniques, build your resume, and practice answering common BPO interview questions. Get tips on professional presentation.

Day 10

Assessment & Graduation

Complete your final assessment, receive your certificate of completion, and learn about job placement opportunities. Top performers receive direct endorsement to Sutherland.
