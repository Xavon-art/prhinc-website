Our Training Programs

Comprehensive BPO training designed to launch your career

Flagship Program

10-Day Call Center Training

Our flagship program is a comprehensive, hands-on training experience designed to prepare you for a successful career in the BPO industry. Over 10 intensive days, you'll learn everything from professional communication to live call simulation.

Duration: 10 Days
Cost: 100% Free
Allowance: P2,000
Location: Brgy. Singalat, Palayan City

Training Modules

Day-by-day breakdown of what you'll learn

Day 1-2

Introduction to BPO

Learn about the BPO industry, career opportunities, and what employers look for in candidates.

  • Industry overview and career paths
  • Company structures and client types
  • Workplace culture and expectations
  • Professional grooming standards
Day 3-4

Professional Communication

Develop the communication skills needed to succeed in a call center environment.

  • English proficiency enhancement
  • Accent neutralization techniques
  • Professional phone etiquette
  • Active listening skills
Day 5-6

Customer Service Excellence

Master the art of delivering exceptional customer service over the phone.

  • Customer needs assessment
  • Problem-solving techniques
  • De-escalation strategies
  • Empathy and rapport building
Day 7-8

Call Handling Skills

Learn how to handle different types of calls professionally and efficiently.

  • Inbound call management
  • Outbound call techniques
  • Call documentation
  • CRM system basics
Day 9

Mock Calling Sessions

Practice your skills with realistic mock calls and receive immediate feedback.

  • Role-playing scenarios
  • Real-time feedback
  • Performance assessment
  • Improvement coaching
Day 10

Assessment & Certification

Complete your final assessment and receive your certificate of completion.

  • Final skills assessment
  • Certificate distribution
  • Job placement guidance
  • Direct endorsement to Sutherland
