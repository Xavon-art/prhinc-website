Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our training program

Yes, absolutely! Our 10-day call center training program is completely free. There are no hidden fees, no tuition costs, and no charges for training materials. We also provide free lunch daily throughout the training.

Our training is open to all eligible Filipino citizens, particularly residents of Nueva Ecija and surrounding areas. You must be at least 18 years old, have completed at least high school education, and be willing to commit to the full 10-day training schedule. No prior call center experience is required.

To join our training program, you need: (1) A valid government-issued ID, (2) At least high school education (but college level or graduates are also welcome), (3) Basic English communication skills, (4) Willingness to learn and commitment to attend all 10 days of training, and (5) Completed registration form which you can fill out on our website or in person.

The training runs for 10 consecutive days (excluding weekends). Sessions typically run from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM at Brgy. Caimito, Palayan City. New batches start regularly, so check our website or social media for the latest schedule.

Absolutely! Our top-performing graduates receive direct endorsement to Sutherland, one of the largest BPO companies globally. We also provide resume building assistance, interview preparation, and connect graduates with other partner BPO companies. Our employment rate among graduates is over 80%.

We welcome trainees from all over Nueva Ecija and neighboring provinces. Training is held at Brgy. Caimito, Palayan City.

PRHInc stands out because: (1) Our training is 100% free with no hidden costs, (2) We provide direct job endorsement to Sutherland, (3) Our trainers have extensive BPO industry experience, (4) We offer hands-on mock calling sessions with real-time feedback, (5) We provide practical benefits like free lunch, and (6) We have strong community support and industry partnerships.

Once you become a certified agent after completing our training, starting salaries for call center agents in the Philippines typically range from P15,000 to P25,000 per month, depending on the company and account type. Night shift positions often come with additional allowances. With experience and good performance, you can advance to team leader or supervisor positions with significantly higher salaries.

Registering is easy! You can fill out our online registration form on this website. Simply click the "Register Now" button, fill in your details (name, email, phone, address, educational attainment), and submit. You'll receive a confirmation email with further instructions. You can also register in person at our training center in Brgy. Caimito, Palayan City.
