Our Partners & Supporters

Organizations that make our mission possible

City Government of Palayan

Under the leadership of Mayor Viandrei Nicole J. Cuevas, the city government provides crucial support for our training programs, including facilities and resources.

TESDA

The Technical Education and Skills Development Authority accredits our training programs and supports national competency certification for our graduates.

Sutherland

One of the largest BPO companies globally, Sutherland partners with us for direct job endorsement of our top-performing graduates.

Palayan City Institute of Technology

PCIT serves as the educational foundation for our training programs, providing TESDA-accredited courses since 2013.
